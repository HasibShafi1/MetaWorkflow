Integrated Meta Strategy Workflow

How Social Media Managers & Meta Marketers work together for organic & paid success

Social Media Manager

Phase 1: Strategy & Planning

Define business goals, audience personas & overall content + ad calendar.

  • Research audience & competitors
  • Map organic & paid campaigns together
  • Set up workflow tools
Meta Marketer

Phase 2: Campaign Setup

Meta Marketer ensures tracking & ad structure is ready for performance-based campaigns.

  • Install Meta Pixel & Conversion API
  • Build Saved/Custom/Lookalike audiences
  • Choose campaign objectives (Awareness, Sales, Leads)
Social Media Manager

Phase 3: Content Creation

Produce high-quality Reels, Stories, & Feed posts optimized for Meta platforms.

  • Design visuals & write compelling captions
  • Use AI tools for ideas & copy
  • Repurpose top-performing content into ads
Meta Marketer

Phase 4: Scheduling & Publishing

Align organic publishing with paid ad launches for maximum impact.

  • SMM schedules posts at optimal times
  • Meta Marketer launches paid ads
  • Maintain consistency across formats
Social Media Manager

Phase 5: Community Engagement

Foster trust by interacting with followers & addressing customer needs fast.

  • Reply to comments & DMs within 1 hour
  • Monitor brand mentions & discussions
  • Move sensitive discussions to private chat
Meta Marketer

Phase 6: Performance Monitoring

Analyze organic & paid metrics together to find what drives real results.

  • SMM reviews reach & engagement trends
  • Meta Marketer checks CTR, ROAS, CPA
  • Share insights to improve both strategies
SMM + Meta Marketer

Phase 7: Optimization & Scaling

Turn insights into action by refining content & ad strategy for better ROI.

  • Boost top organic posts as ads
  • Retarget engaged audiences
  • Scale winning creatives, pause weak ones

Step 1

Campaign Strategy

Choose the right objective (Sales, Leads, etc.)

Use Pixel data, Custom & Lookalike Audiences

Step 3

Design scroll-stopping visuals & persuasive copy

Step 4

Optimization & Scaling
